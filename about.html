About KalamHub

Why we built this, how it works, and what your purchase supports.

Our Mission

Preserving Work. Empowering Students.

Every year, ECE students build incredible projects — complex circuits, smart sensors, embedded systems. But too often, that work disappears after evaluation.

Kalam Hub preserves those projects and their components, making them available as reusable modules for the next batch of students and builders. Knowledge built here stays here.

How It Works

Simple. Transparent. Useful.

01

Browse Components

Filter by category, type, or search for what you need. Each component has a detailed description and preview.

02

Choose Your Access

Temporary access is free — great for learning and referencing. Permanent access includes full files for your own build.

03

Get & Build

Receive your component files immediately. Use them in your project, or study the design to learn the concept.

04

Support Kalam Hub

Revenue from permanent licenses goes directly toward Kalam Hub resources, lab equipment, and student initiatives.
